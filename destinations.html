Top Destinations

Plan routes through forests, beaches, tea country, and heritage cities.

Sundarbans

The Sundarbans is the world's largest mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Boat journeys through its waterways reveal dense forests, wildlife, quiet channels, and a powerful sense of wilderness.

Visitors usually travel with trained guides and licensed operators because the area is protected and remote. A responsible trip supports conservation and helps preserve this remarkable coastal ecosystem.

Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is famous for its long sandy beach, fishing boats, seafood, and relaxed sea views. It is one of Bangladesh's most popular holiday destinations for families and groups.

Travelers can enjoy sunrise walks, nearby island trips, local markets, and coastal food. For more protected nature, many visitors add Himchari or Inani Beach to the same route.
