A Land of Rivers and Stories

Bangladesh is shaped by rivers, monsoon seasons, fertile plains, and a long tradition of trade and culture. From the Sundarbans in the southwest to tea gardens in Sylhet, the country offers visitors a wide range of natural beauty and local experiences.

Travelers can explore ancient mosques, Buddhist heritage sites, lively city neighborhoods, and quiet rural villages. This guide highlights a few places, customs, foods, and practical ideas for planning a memorable trip.

For broader country details, visit the official tourism resource at Beautiful Bangladesh.
