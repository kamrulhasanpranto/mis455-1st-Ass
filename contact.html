Plan Your Visit

Before traveling, make a route that matches the season, distance, and pace you prefer. Bangladesh is compact on the map, but road and river journeys can take time, so a realistic schedule makes the trip more enjoyable.

It is helpful to keep copies of documents, confirm accommodation early during holidays, and check local transport options before moving between cities. Guided tours are useful for protected forests, archaeological sites, and remote river areas.
