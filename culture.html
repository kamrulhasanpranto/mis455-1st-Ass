Culture and Heritage

Meet the music, festivals, crafts, and traditions that shape daily life.

Festivals and Everyday Traditions

Bangladeshi culture is strongly connected to language, literature, music, and seasonal festivals. Pohela Boishakh, the Bengali New Year, brings processions, fairs, traditional food, and colorful clothing into public spaces.

Craft traditions also remain important across the country. Jamdani weaving, pottery, folk painting, and hand-stitched nakshi kantha quilts reflect local skill and identity passed through generations.

Learn more about cultural heritage through UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.
