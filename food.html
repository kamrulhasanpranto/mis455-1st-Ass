Food and Flavors

Rice, fish, spices, sweets, tea, and street snacks define the table.

Meals Worth Trying

Bangladeshi meals often center on rice, fish, lentils, vegetables, and layered spice combinations. Hilsa fish, bhuna dishes, biryani, pitha, and chutneys are common highlights for visitors exploring local restaurants or family meals.

Street food is another major part of the experience. Fuchka, chotpoti, jhal muri, kebabs, tea, and fresh sweets are easy to find in busy neighborhoods and market areas.

For food safety while traveling, the CDC Travelers' Health website has practical health guidance.
